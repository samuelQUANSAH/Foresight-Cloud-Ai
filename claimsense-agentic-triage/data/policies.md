# Policy Knowledge Base

## MRI Lumbar Spine Prior Authorization Policy
MRI lumbar spine may be approved when the patient has persistent back pain with neurological symptoms and has completed conservative therapy such as physical therapy, medication management, or activity modification for at least six weeks.

MRI may require escalation when red flags are missing, symptoms are vague, or documentation does not clearly connect diagnosis, prior treatment, and medical necessity.

## Human Review Policy
Cases should be routed to human review when confidence is below 0.75, documentation is incomplete, protected health information appears unnecessary for the decision, or the recommendation could materially affect patient access to care.

## PII and Compliance Policy
The system should avoid exposing unnecessary personal identifiers. Member IDs may be used for workflow tracking, but clinical recommendations should not reveal unrelated personal information.
